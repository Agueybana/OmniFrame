# OKRs Skill

Use OKRs when the request needs measurable alignment and operating cadence.

Root canvas expectations:
- Objectives are qualitative, directional, and specific to the user's domain.
- Key results are measurable, time-bound, and behavior/outcome based.
- Initiatives are plausible moves that can influence the key results.
- Risks identify what could make the OKR performative or misleading.

Sublayer expectations:
- Owners, cadence, review thresholds, and evidence rules must be explicit.
