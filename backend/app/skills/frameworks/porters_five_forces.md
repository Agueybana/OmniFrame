# Porter's Five Forces Skill

Use Porter's Five Forces when the request depends on industry structure, competitive pressure, substitutes, buyers, suppliers, or profitability.

Root canvas expectations:
- Rivalry, entrants, substitutes, buyer power, and supplier power must be populated with domain-specific actors or actor categories.
- Ratings must be justified by user context and realistic market dynamics.
- Each force should show whether it creates moat, risk, pricing pressure, distribution pressure, or strategy constraint.

Sublayer expectations:
- Evidence options must name concrete market signals.
- Actions must convert structural pressure into positioning, wedge, partnership, pricing, or go-to-market moves.
